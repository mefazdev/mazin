INSECT CONTROL

Insects can be a real menace to our homes and they can be the carriers of insects. To get rid of them it is necessary to hire a professional like MAZIN so that you can ensure the house is free from unwanted guests. Before starting the pest control process it is important to remove the things from the site and store them properly. And after the treatment, it can be reused without a chance of reinfestation.
Things to do before the treatment
Remove and shift all the furniture so that the professionals can get access to the corners and together with which we can protect our stuff from chemical sprays.
Ensure your clothes, jewellery, toys etc are removed and stored in the cabinet. Cover the cabinet corners with tape which are not made of paper and donâ€™t absorb the chemical sprays.
Remove or plastic wrap all the cushions, beds and pillows.
Cover sofas, tables and chairs with plastic wrap.
Some pest removal treatments need the furniture to be shifted.
Clear the dustbin, countertop and all storage utensils in the kitchen.
Remove all the kitchen equipment, water outlets like filters etc. or plastic wrap them.
Unplug your fridge.
Drop your pests to the neighbourhood.
Cover aquariums with tight plastics and turn off the air pumps.
Cover the paintings, flowerpots.
Empty the bathrooms completely.
