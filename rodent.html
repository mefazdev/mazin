HOW TO IDENTIFY RODENT PROBLEMS?
· To identify the rat problem, take a look around your premises, for entrance points, large droppings, evidence of damage and their footprints.
· Garden areas also have to check for underneath sheds, they like to live in compost bins.
· When rats get into the premises, can see broken air bricks, large gaps under and around the door frames.
· Rats are usually found in undisturbed areas like basements
